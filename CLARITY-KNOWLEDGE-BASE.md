## MORPHEUS8 RF MICRONEEDLING

### Treatment Overview
Morpheus8 is a leading skin tightening treatment that targets the deeper layers of the skin using a matrix of microneedles. It delivers RF (radiofrequency) energy below the surface to remodel collagen and elastin, tighten skin, and reduce irregularities.

### How It Works
- Uses matrix of microneedles
- Delivers radiofrequency energy below skin surface
- Remodels collagen and elastin
- Tightens skin
- Reduces irregularities such as fine lines, wrinkles, and acne scars

### Treatment Details
- **Treatment Type:** Radiofrequency
- **Treatment Time:** 30-60 minutes (typically)
- **Downtime:** Minimal
- **Price:** On Consultation
- **Results:** Varied

### Suitable For
- All skin types
- Can be customized for each patient's needs

### Areas That Can Be Treated
- Face (including under-eye area and jowls)
- Neck
- DÃ©colletage
- Abdomen
- Arms
- Thighs
- Knees
- Jawline

### Treatment Process
1. Thorough consultation at Clarity Cosmetics to tailor treatment to skin goals
2. Topical anaesthetic can be applied (not always required)
3. Morpheus8 device delivers controlled energy beneath skin surface
4. Sessions last 30-60 minutes

### Post-Treatment
- **Downtime:** Minimal
- **Side Effects:** Redness, swelling, or mild sensitivity for a few days
- **Results Timeline:**
  - Improvement visible within a few weeks
  - Optimal results develop over 2-3 months as collagen production increases

### Key Benefits
1. **Stimulates Collagen Production** - Boosts natural skin regeneration for long-term improvement
2. **Tightens & Lifts** - Reduces sagging by targeting deeper layers of skin and fat
3. **Contouring** - Particularly effective for double chin and jowls by tightening skin and reducing fat under the skin
4. **Improves Texture & Tone** - Smooths rough patches, acne scars, and fine lines
5. **Minimally Invasive** - Little to no downtime with dramatic results over time
6. **Safe for All Skin Types** - FDA cleared fractional radiofrequency device for soft tissue contraction (first and only)
7. **Natural-Looking Results** - Long-lasting with minimal recovery time

### Pain Level
Generally comfortable with topical anaesthetic, though not always required

### Ideal Candidates
- Those looking to improve skin laxity
- Reduce signs of aging
- Rejuvenate appearance without surgery
- Seeking natural-looking, long-lasting results with minimal recovery time

### FDA Status
First and only FDA cleared fractional radiofrequency device for soft tissue contraction

### Platform Overview
EmpowerRF by InMode is an advanced, non-surgical platform designed to address a range of vaginal and pelvic health concerns.

### Why Consider Vaginal Rejuvenation?
Many women experience changes due to:
- Age
- Menopause
- Childbirth
- Hormonal shifts

### Treatment Approach
Minimally invasive treatments that:
- Enhance vaginal tissue
- Improve pelvic muscle strength
- Restore natural lubrication
- Little to no downtime

### Technology Components

#### 1. **VTone**
- Gentle, nonsurgical intravaginal electrical muscle stimulation (EMS)
- Neuromuscular re-education
- Rehabilitates weak pelvic floor muscles
- Treats stress, urge, and mixed urinary incontinence
- Addresses vaginal laxity and weak pelvic floor muscles

#### 2. **Morpheus8V**
- Fast and efficacious fractional bipolar radiofrequency technology
- 24 pin matrix of thin gold-coated microneedles
- Treatment depth of 3mm
- Electrocoagulation and fractional bulk heating
- Remodels the labia
- Rejuvenates the vaginal canal
- Tightens vaginal canal, increases lubrication
- Renews thickness, strength, and tone in labia and vaginal walls

#### 3. **FormaV**
- Certified for feminine wellness
- Approved for muscle stimulation, relaxation, pain reduction
- Improves blood circulation
- Treats symptoms of Genitourinary Syndrome of Menopause (GSM)
- Tightens vaginal canal
- Promotes collagen restructuring
- Improves vaginal elasticity, sensitivity, and lubrication

#### 4. **Tone** (For Abdominals)
- Non-invasive muscle strengthening
- Low frequency pulses with electrical muscle stimulation (EMS)
- Induces involuntary muscle contractions
- Hands-free applicators
- Restores, sculpts, and refines muscle appearance
- No surgery or downtime

### Advantages Over Surgery
Unlike surgical procedures such as labiaplasty or vaginoplasty, EmpowerRF offers:
- Noticeable results
- No surgical risks
- No extended recovery time
